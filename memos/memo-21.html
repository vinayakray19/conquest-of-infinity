Memo #21

The Inevitable

January 8, 2025

I am neither a financial nor a marketing expert. But I have never liked the way edTech companies like PW are marketing themselves. Especially The Ones with Big Photo of the founder on all marketing ads. The inevitable has arrived. PW by Alakh Pandey sees the loss of approximately 1100 crore in FY24.

My takeaways from such organization are :

  1. Individuals can never replace the power of organization in the long run.
  2. Fake it till you make it is unsustainable.
  3. The bubble has to burst sooner or later.
  4. Product first and marketing next.
  5. Last but not the least, Education and Acting are probably separate domains.

I wish the landslide stops for PW but the edTech industry hasn't been too kind till now to the entrepreneurs. Byju has been the case in point.
