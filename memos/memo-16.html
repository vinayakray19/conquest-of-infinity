Memo #16

A curious Student

October 26, 2024

Student : Is knowledge essential to make money?

Teacher : No dear, absolutely not.

Student : Then, what is needed to make money?

Teacher : Skill set dear.

Student : What skill set sir?

Teacher : Any, that can increase productivity or could help in production and maintenance of society. From barber to manager. Anything.

Student : Then why should I seek Knowledge?

Teacher : Dear, Vidya Dadati Vinayam. That's it. It is for nothing else.

Student : Are books the only way to seek knowledge?

Teacher : No dear, Absolutely not.

Student : So, what are the other ways.

Teacher : Any, that can possibly break you.
