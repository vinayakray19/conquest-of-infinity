Memo #19

The Calm Old Man

January 6, 2025

There was a circular track with large radius. There were many people constantly hustling on that circular track. However, an old man was simply resting in calmness at one of the point on the track. Everyone used to think, why on earth the old man is not moving from its place. Slowly and gradually as the people got frustrated by running on the same track again and again understood the wisdom of the old man.
