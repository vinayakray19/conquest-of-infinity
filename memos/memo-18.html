Memo #18

3-0NZ and Lesson to learn

November 3, 2024

Within two years of time, Rohit Sharma led India to an impeccable run in the 2023 ODI World Cup just to lose the final against the mighty Aussies to a Desperate win from no where in the 2024 T20 world Cup Final against South Africa. While Rohit was just enjoying the time at the peak, lost 3-0 in test series against the least expected team(NZ) at the least Expected place(India).

This life seems to be pretty much similar for everyone. One day you feel as if you are on the top and the next day you don't find a place to hide yourself. I hope Rohit would find a place to hide as he did post 2023 WC final loss.
