Memo #1

The ongoing crisis

June 1, 2020

Suicide and accidental deaths of labours have raised so many questions that is crying for answers from not only the government, but also the so called middle and upper class citizens.

More than two months have passed, when the Honourable Prime Minister made the decision of lock down. Was four hours sufficient for the people to prepare for the lockdown raises the first of its series of questions? More the money as resources we have, less the time it takes to generate or arrange the other resources. Hence, four hours might have been sufficient for the people who receives hefty amount of salary or the one with savings to survive for months and years. But what about the people with penny in hands just as much as to survive for a day or two?
